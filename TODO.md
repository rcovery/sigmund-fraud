Probably I will make an IVF (inverted file index).



- IVF is a french abbreviation, something like "Index de Vecteurs en Fichier inversé".
- IVF is an content based index, which means you will filter by content. In this case, by float numbers.
- The index key is called "centroid", in our case, will be the vector average.
